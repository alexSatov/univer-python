GAME-LINES help
The game will randomly place three color balls on the game board at every round, 
you have to strategically select one ball (click on ball) and move it to the most convenient location (click on target square).
Game goal is to arrange balls of the same colour in a straight lines (every direction). 
5 or more balls on a straight line are removed and points are given(1 removed ball = 1 point)
Preview panel shows colours of the next 3 balls.
