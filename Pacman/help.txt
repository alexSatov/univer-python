########PACMAN########
You play pacman and your job is to eat all the food on the map.
Control:
a - left
d - right
w - up
s - down
e - create a wall behind pacman (-200 scores)
f - attack
Beware of 4 ghosts:
Blinky - red ghost
Inky - blue ghost
Pinky - pink ghost
Clyde - orange ghost
If you get one of the ghosts, he will kill you (minus one life, all lives - 3).
You can kill the ghost with a special weapon.
After that they go to his house to respawn.
Once there, the ghosts come to life and begin to hunt again.
For eating food and killing the ghosts get points.
Forward, put the records !!!
